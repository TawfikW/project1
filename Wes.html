Admissions

Students attending Oxford Academy must reside at all times with their parent/guardian within the boundaries of the Anaheim Union High School District.

Entrance Requirements:

Entrance to Oxford Academy is by application and examination and is limited to students entering 7th 8th or 9th grade. Oxford Academy does not enroll students who have progressed beyond the 9th grade.

Students must live with their parent/guardian within the boundaries of the Anaheim Union High School. Visit the following web site to view a map of our district boundaries:

Map

Testing Process:

Once students have met the entrance requirements, they are invited to take the Oxford Academy Entrance Exam.

The exam is given on a Saturday, and the entire testing process takes approximately four hours.
